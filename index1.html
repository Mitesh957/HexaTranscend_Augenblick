Waste Management and Segregation

"Waste management and segregation involve responsible handling of waste materials to minimize environmental impact. Segregation categorizes waste into recyclable, organic, and non-recyclable types for appropriate disposal methods. Effective waste management practices promote recycling, composting, and proper disposal, contributing to a cleaner and more sustainable environment."
